(Optional) Challenge 6: Analyzing the ABC Sales Dashboard app

Summary:

  • Apply the concepts you learned in this module by answering business questions.
  • Explore the data using the Selections tool and review the data relationships with the Qlik associative engine.
  • Make selections and use the Qlik search capabilities to find answers in the data and generate useful insights.

Module: Overview, selecting and searching data

Qlik Sense app:ABC Sales Dashboard FINAL

Challenge

Use the ABC Sales Dashboard FINAL app to answer the following questions about the data:

  • Question 1: What is the sales share in the EMEA region and how many customers are in this region? Identify the top 3 customers in this region based on sales and the list of products never sold in the region.
  • Question 2: What is the order volume range in North America and South America? Are there any outliers in these regions? If yes, provide the customer name and order volume.
  • Question 3: Including Bruxelles and all the area 400 km around this city, how many customers are in that area, and what is the total sales generated in the last year in this area?
  • Question 4: How many individual orders over 11.5k sales were made and by how many customers? Where are those customers located and what products did they buy in those transactions? For those customers, what is the Margin% on Runner shoes in this last year?
  • Question 5: Don Balon is one of the VIP customers in the company. Identify what products were not sold in Don Balon's country. From those products, can you tell which ones were not sold to any other customers and what is their UnitCost?
Note: The objective of this challenge is to test whether you feel comfortable with the concepts that have been discussed so far in your class. There is no Step-by-Step section in this activity, so you must only rely on the Hints below to understand the tasks you have to execute.
As you are still new to many of these concepts, please don't hesitate to reach out for more guidance (help file, instructor, Qlik Community).

Hints

  • Make selections in the different filter panes, charts, and tables in the sheets, or use the Selection tool to make specific selections.
  • Notice how the Selection tool allows you to review associative insights to discover extra information about the data.
  • Use the Smart search or the Insight Advisor to search for specific values or criteria in the app.
