Create Visualizations with Qlik Sense

Activity 3: Answer data questions by making selections in an app

Summary:

  • Understand the data and find answers to data questions in a Qlik Sense app.
  • Appreciate the Qlik associative engine and learn how to make selections in different areas of the app.
  • Explore the selection states, the Selections menu, and the Selections bar.
  • Work with the Selections tool, learn how to make advanced selections, and review Associative insights.

Module: Overview, selecting and searching data

Qlik Sense app: Vintage movies

Challenge

Use the Vintage Movies app to answer the following questions:

  • Question 1: Within the 1950's, how many movies are there with a rating of 4? How many of them have a length up to 120 minutes?
  • Question 2: How many movies were created between the years 1970 and 1980 with a rating of 4 or 5? Who were the most productive directors and actors in that period?
  • Question 3: During which decades did John Ford direct his movies? How many movies did John Ford direct in the 1950's and 1960's?
  • Question 4: How many movies from the 1950's were not directed by John Ford? Review how many directors were involved on those movies and find out other details about them (using Associative insights).
  • Question 5: Did John Ford and Ava Gardner work together in the 1950's? If yes, which movies?

Hints

  • Use the different sheets in the app to get the required information for the questions.
  • Filter panes, chart axes labels, and table columns can be used to make selections.
  • The Selection toolbar and the Selection menu allow you to make further selections and review current selections.
  • The Selections tool allows you to make selections in all the fields in the app. From this tool you can access the Associative insights panel to generate insights about the selections and the non-selected values based on the selection.

 

Step-by-step guide
  1. Open the Vintage Movies app and navigate to the Movie selector sheet in analysis mode.

    2. Question 1

  2. Select 1950's in the Decade filter pane. Then, in the table, select the value 4 in the Rating column and confirm the selection. Look at the dynamic text on the Titles filter pane. There are 52 titles from the 1950's decade with a rating of 4.
  3. Using the Selections tool icon () at the top right, click the magnifying glass icon for the Length field and type <=120, then press enter to select the values associated in the search. Confirm the selection and click Selections tool () again to close the Selections tool. From those movies in question 1, there are 38 movies that are less than or equal to 2 hours long.

    4. Question 2

  4. Navigate to the Dashboard sheet and clear all selections. Select the segments in the donut chart that correspond with the ratings 4 and 5, and then perform a range selection in the line chart using the horizontal axis for the years 1970-1980. There were 57 movies.
    5. Note: If you don’t see the ratings in the donut chart or the year values in the x-axis, increase the size of your window or view the chart in full screen mode.
  5. Look at the bar charts to find out who was the most productive director and actor in those movies. Woody Allen and Donald Sutherland were the director and actor that were most productive during that period in those movies with ratings of 4 and 5.

    6. Question 3

  6. Clear all selections and select John Ford using the Number of movies by directors bar chart in the middle of this sheet. Look at the values on the bar chart at the left. John Ford directed his movies from the 1920's to the 1960's.
  7. Select the decades 1950's and 1960's using the bar chart on the left. John Ford directed 23 movies during those decades.

    8. Note: The selected fields are displayed in the Selections toolbar. You can click on the Decade selections to see the selected values. The selected decades display in green and the rest of the decades appear in light gray and dark gray. Looking at the decades you have selected, explain why the remaining decades are displayed on a light gray or dark gray background.

    Question 4

  8. Using the Selection toolbar on the top, click the Decade filter, and deselect 1960's, so the only remaining selected decade is 1950's. Then, using the Selection toolbar, select the Director filter. You can see that John Ford is the only selection. Click the Selection menu ¥ and click Select alternative to select all the directors related to the selected decade excluding John Ford.

  9. Look at the Number of movies KPI on the dashboard. There are 309 movies associated with the selection, but this number is not the correct answer. Movies can be directed by more than one director. In this case, the movie called Mister Roberts is directed by John Ford and Mervyn LeRoy and this movie is being included in the count. You can see the details in the Movie Selector sheet. In order to get the correct answer, you need to first exclude the movies directed by John Ford.

  10. In the Dashboard sheet, step back in the selections using the < button in the Selection toolbar. The selected values are John Ford and 1950's. Then click the Selections icon to display the Selections tool.
  11. Click on the Title field, and using the Selection menu ¥, click Select possible to select all the movies directed by John Ford in the 1950's.
  12. Clear the selections in the Director field. Then click on the Title field again, and using the Selection menu¥, click Select excluded.
  13. Review the Associative insights panel on the right. If this panel is not visible, click Explore selections to display it. Then click Generate insights. The insights cards appear in the panel. From the drop down box, select the Measure: Number of movies. Then click on the first card to review the details: 308 movies.
  14. Review the cards on the right, under the Insights from excluded values area. You can add new fields to review insights from the Add a dimension button at the bottom. Select the Director card and click to select that card from the Insights from excluded values section to see that in total there are 133 directors (21.8%) related with the selection that created a total of 843 movies across all decades.

    15. Question 5

  15. Click Selections to hide the Selection tool and return to the sheet. Step back in the selections using the < button in the Selection toolbar to see all the titles directed by John Ford in the 1950's selected again.
  16. Scroll down in the Number of movies by actor bar chart on the right until you can see Ava Gardner. Select the bar corresponding with Ava Gardner. You can see John Ford and Ava Gardner worked together in 1 movie. Now open the Selections tool to see the Title field. The movie is Mogambo.
